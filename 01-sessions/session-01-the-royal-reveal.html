Session 01 - The Royal Reveal

Session Note: The Royal Reveal and Downtime Dynamics

Current Location Hub

Emberwood Village
Key Sites: The Red Lion Hotel, Buckledown Row and the Bark and Buzzard tavern.

Narrative Acts

Act I: Cosmic and Draconic Visions and The Pale Man Unveiled

  • The Black Hole Vision: Sable awakens deeply shaken from a nightmare: a black hole tearing the world apart.

  • The Academy Consultation: Sable brings the vision to Eldrick at his study. He identifies it not as a simple nightmare but as a potential localized planar singularity tied to the anomalous fluctuations at the heart of the Drakkenheim crater. He instructs her to report immediately if the visions begin occurring while she is awake, and in a telling slip, refers to her as a "viable subject."

  • The Dragon Prophecy: Krenzo shares his own vision: Arganoth, the Golden Wing set against a burning field, somehow tied to Lightbringer Santanael. Consulting Lightbringer Santanael directly, Krenzo learns that the Lightbringer's deep-city research has converged on The Pale Man: a horrific creator of mutated creatures who has been kidnapping children from the surrounding area.

  • The True Identity: The party connects the dots. The figure known publicly as Friedrich von Liechten and The Pale Man are one and the same. Lightbringer Santanael proposes a mutual intelligence-sharing pact going forward.

Act II: Royal Bombshell at the Lantern Encampment

  • Seeking Faction Eyes: The party rides north of Emberwood Village to the secure encampment of the Hooded Lanterns. Clank secures an audience with Ansom Lang and his sister, Petra Lang. The party delivers a massive intelligence drop to the siblings: Queen Lenore is alive deep within the Haze.

  • Seeking Faction Eyes: The party rides north to the secure encampment of the Hooded Lanterns. Clank secures an audience with Ansom Lang and his sister Petra Lang, and the party delivers a staggering intelligence drop: Queen Lenore is alive, deep within the Haze, guarded by a contingent of specialized handmaidens. Her mind is fractured and contaminated by Delirium. The revelation visibly shakes both siblings and immediately raises the geopolitical stakes of the War of Succession.

  • The Missing Son: Before departing, Lily raises the matter of her missing son, Ash. Ansom Lang confirms that Ash is operating inside the city out of a base he calls the "Wolf Den."

Act III: A Month of Downtime, Tavern Wagers, and Local Brawls

  • The Buckledown Excursion: Lily and Sable spend an evening cutting loose on Buckledown Row, trading banter with Lorenzo and navigating the rowdy atmosphere of Queen's Men territory without incident.

  • The Information Game: At the tavern, Clank sits down with Blackjack Mel, leader of the Queen's Men, for a tense coded exchange. Blackjack Mel dangled information regarding Clank's older brother Soros, who is reportedly surviving on the outskirts of town with connections to the Followers of the Fallen Flame.

  • Druidic Experiments: Krenzo partners with Zoya to study the physiological effects of Delirium exposure in a controlled setting. Zoya mentions Moragon, a powerful reclusive druid who vanished into the Haze twenty-five years ago.

  • The Barroom Blitz: A brawl breaks out in the bar, featuring the unexpected entry of a squirrel known simply as Chitters.

  • The Looming Threat: As the month closes we learn that the Iron Accord is actively marching toward the region.

Mechanical and Character Tracking

  • Sable notes a change in Clank's appearance: strange purple leaves sprouting through his shoulder plate.

  • Krenzo spends the month training under Zoya, while helping the people of Emberwood Village with farming and animal husbandry tasks.

  • Clank trains with Ansom Lang in the use of greatswords, swapping Shield Master for Great Weapon Master.

  • Lilly has gained proficiency in Performance after her night in Buckledown Row.

Notable NPC Interactions

  • Ansom Lang and Petra Lang (Hooded Lanterns): Siblings and faction leaders who reacted with profound shock and heightened political urgency to the confirmation of Queen Lenore's corrupted survival.

  • Blackjack Mel (Queen's Men): Maintained total calculated poise during an information game, dangling knowledge of family lineages and missing siblings.

  • Aldor the Immense: A wealthy, bizarre, and highly magical merchant.

  • Zoya: Facilitated hazardous medical and environmental research into raw Delirium mutations.

  • Chitters: An unexpected martial threat who completely dominated a casual barroom brawl with explosive natural strikes.

  • Ash: Lilly's missing son who is currently scouting in Drakkenheim.

  • Lorenzo: a charming goblin that Lilly seeks to woo.

Quest Log

  • **Main Thread: The Mad Sovereign
    • Status: Advanced. The Hooded Lanterns have been officially informed that Queen Lenore is alive but mentally contaminated deep within the Haze. A high-level audience with Lord Commander Elias Drexel is being arranged.

  • Faction Thread: Hunting The Pale Man
    • Status: Advanced. The party shared the true identity of Friedrich von Liechten with Lightbringer Santanael. The current objective is to locate his central stronghold and disrupt his flesh-crafting operations.

  • Personal Thread: Finding Ash
    • Status: Active. Received confirmation from the Lanterns that Lily's son is currently operating out of Drakkenheim, from a location he calls the "Wolf's Den".

  • Personal Thread: Tracking Soros
    • Status: Active. Blackjack Mel confirmed that Soros is alive and hiding on the far outskirts of the city with unknown figures.

  • Regional Threat: Arrival of the Iron Accord
    • Status: New Hook. Heavy scouting reports indicate the imminent, dangerous arrival of the Iron Accord into the local territory.
